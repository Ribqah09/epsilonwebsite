ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Epsilon Smart Solution is the evolution of Printed Circuits – Pakistan's first local manufacturer of double-sided PCBs, established in the late 1990s. Today, Epsilon Smart Solution is a modern technology company providing smart industrial and commercial solutions in automation, energy monitoring, and predictive maintenance. We serve factories, pharma labs, energy plants, and defense units across Pakistan.
