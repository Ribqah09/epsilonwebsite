About Epsilon Smart Solution

Epsilon Smart Solution is the evolution of Printed Circuits – Pakistan's first local manufacturer of double-sided PCBs, established in the late 1990s. Today, Epsilon Smart Solution is a modern technology company providing smart industrial and commercial solutions in automation, energy monitoring, and predictive maintenance. We serve factories, pharma labs, energy plants, and defense units across Pakistan.

ENERGY MONITORING

Real-time energy tracking with voltage, current, power factor analysis to reduce wastage and optimize efficiency.

MOTOR MONITORING

Predictive maintenance with current, temperature & vibration sensors for maximum uptime and motor life.

INDUSTRIAL AUTOMATION

PLC programming, SCADA systems, and custom control panels for manufacturing and process industries.

PRODUCTION MONITORING

Real-time OEE tracking, throughput analysis, and workflow optimization for maximum productivity.

PREDICTIVE MAINTENANCE

ML-based failure prediction and condition monitoring to reduce unplanned downtime and extend asset life.
